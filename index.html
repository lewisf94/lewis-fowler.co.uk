About Me

Introduction

I am a Mechanical Engineer with a passion for design, robotics, and thermal systems. I hold an MEng in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Glasgow and currently work at Boyd as a Design Engineer, developing custom cold plates for clients in the space and automotive industries. During university, I partnered with Rolls-Royce for my final-year project, designing a novel high-performance heat exchanger. My interest in robotics has grown through Formula Student and personal projects, many of which are featured below.
